The Problem

Circuit breaker panels are plagued by inaccurate, outdated descriptions. The most common solution — a penciled-in paper register on the panel door — is cramped, hard to read, and almost never updated. There's no record of who made changes or when.

The Solution

MyBreakerPanel gives every panel a digital register — unlimited description space per breaker, a full change history, and instant access via a QR code and Panel ID. No more guesswork. No more illegible pencil marks.

Features

Digital Panel Register

Label every breaker with unlimited text — no more pencil scrawls or cramped paper registers.

QR Code Access

Scan the QR code on your panel door to open the app, then enter your Panel ID to access the register from any phone, tablet, or laptop.

Full Change History

Every update is logged with a date and contact info — know who changed what and when.

Printable Layout

Print a clean, formatted register to keep inside the panel door as a quick backup.
